Methodology
01
DefineCo-define the operational problem and research question with government and industry partners.
02
Build & TestDevelop and test the approach on a fit-for-purpose operational site or dataset, with operators alongside researchers.
03
ValidateEvaluate rigorously. Not everything will work, and that is accepted. Null results have value too — rigour over optimism.
04
OperationaliseAdapt and deploy what works. The implementation pathway is planned from day one, not retrofitted.
Research context
V
VisionTo make Australia's transport networks among the most intelligent and responsive in the world, where research and operations advance together rather than apart.
M
MissionThe bridge between transport research and live network operations — building data infrastructure, algorithms, and decision-support tools grounded in operational reality.
The research problems
▲
Boundary fragmentation. Road and public transport planning run in separate silos. Motorway and arterial control systems are uncoordinated.
◆
Tacit knowledge loss. Decades of operational expertise held by experienced engineers with no scalable transfer mechanism.
■
Research-to-operations gap. Strong research exists, but the pathway from publication to operational deployment is slow and rarely built in from the start.
Published and deployed research
OPTIMADeployed
Motorway algorithm auto-calibration · TMR
Automated calibration of STREAMS motorway algorithms with knowledge-transfer dashboards making algorithm behaviour transparent to operators.
S-curve evaluationTransport Research Record
Before-and-after evaluation · co-published with TMR
Quantifies the value of a network operational change; now standard evaluation methodology in Queensland.
ANSPAOngoing
Arterial performance · multimodal fusion
Fuses STREAMS, Bluetooth, probe, AFC GoCard, and GTFS data into a multimodal arterial performance diagnostic. Targeted for operational integration; not yet in live operations.
WiSe / WRVSLIn development
Weather-responsive variable speed limits
Real-time crash-risk modelling with BoM radar and RWIS to trigger proactive speed management before conditions deteriorate.
GLiVe & SPARCDeployed
Real-time transit + corridor metrics · BCC & TransLink
25 GB/day transit monitoring at 2-10s resolution; SPARC delivers Movement and Place aligned corridor performance with BCC.
