Proven tools and deployments
A decade of delivered work, in Queensland operations today.
OPTIMADeployed
Motorway algorithm calibration · with TMR
Automated calibration of Queue Detection and Queue Protection algorithms. Delivers dashboards that make algorithm behaviour transparent to operations staff.
Outcome: calibration time reduced; algorithm performance measurably improved
WiSe / WRVSLIn development
Weather-responsive variable speed limits · with TMR
Real-time VSL combining BoM radar, RWIS sensors, and crash-risk modelling. Acts proactively before conditions deteriorate, not reactively after.
ANSPAOngoing
Arterial performance monitoring · with TMR / TransLink
Six diagnostic questions, five dashboard types, fusing detector, Bluetooth, probe, and transit data. Targeted for operational integration 2025/26; not yet in live operations.
S-curve evaluationPublished
Before-and-after evaluation · co-published with TMR in Transport Research Record
Quantifies the operational value of a network change; now the standard evaluation methodology across Queensland operations.
SPARC & GLiVeDeployed
Corridor metrics + real-time transit · BCC & TransLink
SPARC delivers Movement and Place aligned corridor performance for BCC. GLiVe tracks buses, CityCats and trains at 2-10s resolution, 25 GB/day.
