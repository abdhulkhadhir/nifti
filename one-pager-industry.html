Proven tools and deployments
A decade of delivered work, in Queensland operations today.
OPTIMADeployed
Motorway algorithm calibration · with TMR
Automated calibration of Queue Detection and Queue Protection algorithms. Delivers dashboards that make algorithm behaviour transparent, enabling knowledge transfer across operations staff.
Outcome: Calibration time reduced; algorithm performance measurably improved
WiSe / WRVSLIn development
Weather-responsive variable speed limits · with TMR
Real-time VSL combining BoM radar, RWIS sensors, and crash-risk modelling. Acts proactively before conditions deteriorate, not reactively after incidents occur.
ANSPAOngoing
Arterial performance monitoring · with TMR / TransLink
Six diagnostic questions, five dashboard types. Fuses detector, Bluetooth, probe, and transit data into one arterial performance picture. Targeted for operational integration 2025/26; not yet in live operations.
S-curve evaluationPublished
Before-and-after evaluation · co-published with TMR in Transport Research Record
Quantifies the operational value of a network change. Co-authored with TMR engineers; now the standard evaluation methodology across Queensland operations.
SPARC & GLiVeDeployed
Person-centric corridor metrics + real-time transit · BCC & TransLink
SPARC delivers Movement and Place aligned corridor performance for BCC. GLiVe tracks all buses, CityCats and trains at 2-10s resolution, processing 25 GB of transit data daily.

The operating model
01
Connected data foundation. One operational picture across detectors, transit, Bluetooth, probe trajectories, and external feeds.
02
Algorithms and optimisation. Proactive, multimodal control across the motorway-arterial boundary as one coordinated cascade.
03
InteliAgent decision support. Live data and institutional knowledge delivered to operators at the moment of decision, cutting onboarding time for new staff by preserving expertise as experienced engineers turn over.
Brisbane 2032 opportunity
The Games will put Queensland's transport network under extreme, globally visible demand. The tools and capability built through NiFTi are the infrastructure that makes 2032 manageable, and that outlasts it as a permanent capability uplift.
