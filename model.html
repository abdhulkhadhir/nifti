The operating model

Three layers, working as one system.

The hub is built around three layers that map onto how a network is actually run. Each already has working pieces, delivered with our partners. The frontier is making them work together.

Layer01

Connected data foundation

One operational picture across modes, agencies, and sensor sources, including signals from the systems transport interacts with. The silo problem solved at the data level.

  • Loops & detectors
  • Bluetooth
  • Probe / trajectory
  • Transit (GTFS)
  • External feeds
Layer02

Algorithms and optimisation

Proactive, multimodal control that is movement-and-place aware and works across the motorway and arterial boundary, rather than optimising each in isolation.

  • Motorway cascade
  • Arterial coordination
  • M&P-aware targets
  • Weather-responsive
Layer03

InteliAgent decision support

Live data, models, and institutional knowledge brought to operators of any experience level, so the right insight arrives at the moment of decision and nothing is lost as the workforce turns over.

  • Operator support
  • LLM / agentic
  • Institutional memory
  • Scenario testing

Smarter at the assess, the decide, and the act.

The three layers map onto INO's own operating cycle. Layer 1 makes assessment sharper, Layer 2 makes action smarter, and Layer 3 makes decisions better, at every step.
