    Layer01

    Connected data foundation

    One operational picture across modes, agencies, and sensor sources, including signals from the systems transport interacts with. The silo problem solved at the data level.

    • Loops & detectors
    • Bluetooth
    • Probe / trajectory
    • Transit (GTFS)
    • External feeds
    In practice
    • STREAMS (~7,500 loop detectors across Queensland) fused with GLiVe (25 GB/day transit positions) and 2,200+ Bluetooth scanners into a single operational feed
    • AFC GoCard tap-on/tap-off data and probe vehicle trajectories layered on top for multimodal completeness
    • Weather and external event feeds integrated to trigger responsive algorithm adjustments in Layer 2
    Layer02

    Algorithms and optimisation

    Proactive, multimodal control that is movement-and-place aware and works across the motorway and arterial boundary, rather than optimising each in isolation.

    • Motorway cascade
    • Arterial coordination
    • M&P-aware targets
    • Weather-responsive
    In practice
    • OPTIMA Deployed - automated motorway control algorithm calibration, removing manual tuning burden and making algorithm behaviour transparent to operators
    • ANSPA Ongoing - multimodal arterial performance measurement, fusing signal, probe, transit and Bluetooth data across SEQ corridors
    • WiSe / WRVSL In development - real-time weather-responsive variable speed limits that adapt proactively before conditions deteriorate
    Layer03

    InteliAgent decision support

    Live data, models, and institutional knowledge brought to operators of any experience level, so the right insight arrives at the moment of decision and nothing is lost as the workforce turns over.

    • Operator support
    • LLM / agentic
    • Institutional memory
    • Scenario testing
    In practice
    • InteliAgent In development - an LLM-backed agentic system that surfaces precedents, explains model behaviour, and supports scenario testing
    • Designed so that expertise held by experienced traffic engineers is captured, not lost when staff move on
    • Queries Layer 1 data and Layer 2 algorithm outputs in real time to deliver grounded, explainable recommendations

    Smarter at the assess, the decide, and the act.

    The three layers map onto INO's own operating cycle. Layer 1 makes assessment sharper, Layer 2 makes action smarter, and Layer 3 makes decisions better, at every step.
