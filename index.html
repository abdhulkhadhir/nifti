About the hub
A standing home for transport intelligence.
NiFTi is a proposed ARC Research Hub that places government, industry, and academia at the same table to solve operational transport problems together. Not a one-off project. Not a single paper. A permanent, co-owned research-to-operations bridge for Queensland's road network.

The NiFTi innovation cycle
From operational pain point to deployed solution.
01 · Define
Define the problem
Co-define the operational problem and what success looks like, jointly with industry. We start where the pain is.
02 · Build & Test
Research, build, test
Develop the approach and test it on a fit-for-purpose site or dataset, with operators alongside researchers.
03 · Validate
Validate honestly
Evaluate rigorously. Not everything will work and that is accepted. Null results have value too. Rigour over optimism.
04 · Operationalise
Move it into operations
Adapt and deploy what works. The implementation path is planned from day one, not retrofitted at the end.
